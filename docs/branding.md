Choice(s):

- `ventapilot`
   - `venta` comes from Spanish `ventas` meaning `sales`. (So it directly connects to benta / selling / negosyo / revenue.)
   - `pilot` means:
   - a guide
   - someone (or something) that controls or assists navigation
   - in tech, often used to mean an AI assistant or system that helps you run something

Combined meaning: `VentaPilot`:

    - 👉 “Your sales co-pilot”
    - 👉 “An assistant that helps you run your store”
    - 👉 “A system that guides your business decisions”
